Graffiti: Theory

Graffiti aims to give all users more autonomy over their social applications. This page is a theoretical overview of the project and the principles that guide it's design. If you want to dive straight in to using existing Graffiti applications, head over to the application garden instead.

The sections below explore the following questions: Why should Graffiti matter, both to you as an individual and to society as a whole? How does Graffiti work on a high level? How does Graffiti compare to other projects like Mastodon, Matrix, and Solid?
